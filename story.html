Our Love Story​

To be quite honest, we are unsure of the precise moment or day that we fell in love. But falling in love with each other took some time for us. Our first move opened the doors to a hundred other conversations. We were getting to know one another better as we discussed our likes and dislikes, what makes each other happy or furious, and the events that have happened in our lives. We have constantly communicated and have had lengthy phone conversations. We discussed our past together. We were both good listeners and our conversations created a soft corner in our heart.

We have realized that we share a lot of similarities after talking about our likes and dislikes, and priorities. Basically we ended up being best friends. Our concept of arranged marriage was the same that we won't fall in love within a short time. But for us, it was different.

We felt every bit of attention and importance as the days went by. Our top concern has shifted. The emotions were eerily divine but yet incredibly odd. Something we had never felt before.
“What is this that we are feeling? Is it love? Are we in love? Are we really going to have our arranged marriage love story?”
